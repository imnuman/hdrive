Motor Options

BLDC & INTEGRATED

Chinese manufacturers now offer Maxon-quality BLDC motors at a fraction of the cost. For robotic arms, integrated actuators (motor + planetary + driver + encoder) provide the best value and simplify integration. Frameless motors are ideal for custom builds with harmonic drives.

Integrated Actuators (Motor + Planetary + Driver + Encoder)

Complete plug-and-play solutions. These include planetary gearboxes (9:1 to 35:1), FOC drivers, and high-resolution encoders. Output torque values shown. For harmonic drive output, multiply by your harmonic ratio (e.g., 40Nm × 10:1 additional = 400Nm theoretical, limited by harmonic capacity).

Harmonic Drive Suppliers

STRAIN WAVE GEARS

Harmonic drives (strain wave gears) provide zero-backlash, high-ratio reduction in a compact package. Japanese originals (Harmonic Drive AG) are premium but expensive. Chinese alternatives from Laifual, Leaderdrive, and others now offer 80-90% of the performance at 20-30% of the cost.

Size Selection Guide

Quality Tier Explanation

Premium (Harmonic Drive AG, Nidec)

<1 arcmin backlash, certified specs, proven 10,000+ hour lifespan. Use for: Production robots, medical devices, aerospace.

High (Laifual, Leaderdrive)

≤10 arcsec backlash, consistent quality, 5,000+ hour typical life. Use for: Prosumer robots, research, small-batch production.

Mid-High (Arcsec, PICEA)

≤20 arcsec backlash, decent QC, variable lifespan. Use for: Prototypes, hobby projects, non-critical applications.

Budget (QXCIVR, generic)

20+ arcsec backlash, inconsistent QC, shorter lifespan. Use for: Learning, proof-of-concept, disposable prototypes.

Component Selection Guide

DECISION MATRIX

Choose your configuration based on your primary requirements. All recommendations assume ~40Nm output torque for main arm joints.

Budget Prototype (<$300/joint)

RobStride 04
At ~$280, the RobStride 04 delivers 40Nm rated torque with integrated driver, encoder, and 9:1 planetary. Best value for getting a robot moving quickly. Use directly for lower-precision applications or add harmonic for high precision.

High Precision Build (<10 arcsec)

ZeroErr eRob90 OR Custom (RI80 + Laifual FSG-20)
eRob90 ($500-1500) for fastest integration with ±7 arcsec repeatability. Custom build with CubeMars RI80 (~$250) + Laifual FSG-20-100 (~$300) + encoder (~$50) = ~$600 total, but more flexible and repairable.

Quick Availability (Need it now)

MyActuator RMD-X10 S2
Available from RobotShop with US shipping. Mature product with good documentation and community support. $550-650 for 50Nm rated. Not the cheapest, but reliable supply chain.

Maximum Torque/$ Ratio

RobStride 04 + QXCIVR PG20-100
RobStride 04 (~$280) driving a QXCIVR harmonic (~$150) = ~$430 for up to 400Nm theoretical (limited by harmonic capacity). Budget option for learning or disposable prototypes. QC varies on the harmonic - order extras.

Production Robot (Reliability First)

Laifual FSG-20 + CubeMars RI80 + Custom Housing
Laifual harmonics are OEM-grade with consistent quality. Combined with a quality frameless motor, this configuration offers the best balance of performance, cost, and long-term reliability. Plan for ~$500-700/joint including housing and encoder.
